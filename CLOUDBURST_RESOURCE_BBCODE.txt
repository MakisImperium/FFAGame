[B]Database Outage Protection (MySQL Mode)[/B]

If [ICODE]storageBackend[/ICODE] is set to [ICODE]MYSQL[/ICODE] and the database becomes unreachable:
[LIST=1]
[*]Stats service is marked unavailable immediately.
[*]All arenas are forced to Offline.
[*]Active arena sessions are ended safely.
[*]Reconnect is attempted [B]3 times[/B] in [B]15-second intervals[/B].
[*]If all attempts fail, plugin advises switching to JSON and disables itself.
[/LIST]

If MySQL reconnects in time, stats are restored. Arenas remain offline until manually set live by admin.
